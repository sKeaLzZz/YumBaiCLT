Bringing the Streets of Phnom Penh to NC

Started in 2024, YumBai CLT is on a mission to introduce the bold, aromatic, and savory flavors of Cambodian street food to Charlotte and the surrounding areas. "Yum Bai" translates to "Eat Rice" or "Let's Eat" in Khmer, and that's exactly what we want to share with our community.


From our signature lemongrass skewers to refreshing papaya salads, everything is made from scratch using traditional family recipes. Whether you're in South End, NoDa, or out in the suburbs, keep an eye out for the truck!

On The Grill

Sach Ko Ang $12

Classic Cambodian beef skewers marinated in kroeung (lemongrass paste), grilled over open flames. Served with pickled papaya.

Bok Lahong $10

Spicy green papaya salad pounded in a mortar and pestle with tomatoes, long beans, peanuts, lime, and fish sauce.

Slaab Moan Baok $14

Crispy stuffed chicken wings filled with glass noodles, minced pork, and traditional spices. Served with sweet chili dipping sauce.

Lort Cha $13

Stir-fried short rice noodles (pin noodles) with bean sprouts, chives, topped with a fried egg and our signature sweet soy sauce.

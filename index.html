Our Story

Founded in 2024, YumBai CLT is dedicated to sharing the rich, bold, and authentic tastes of Cambodia with the Charlotte community. Whether you're craving our signature beef sticks or our perfectly paired combos, we serve up street food exactly how it's meant to be enjoyed.
